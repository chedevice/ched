Product Line

Self-Powered Battery

The Seed (Self-Power Battery) is an electrical energy generating device that uses 12 watts to generate a large amount of energy and at the same time repower itself by subtracting 10 watts from the energy it's generating. It also provides electrical energy to other devices that requires electrical energy to function, this includes inverters, phones and other electronics.

Self-Powered Electric Generating Device

The Self-Powered Electric Generating Device is an inverter that does not require solar panels or batteries to function. Instead, it’s powered by the Self-Powered Battery.


Problem it Solves:

Pollution is still generated with some renewable energy. Renewable energies are cleaner than most fossil fuels, but “cleaner” and “clean” are very different terms. Fuel like biomass still burns waste products and emits wastes that pollutes the atmosphere. This includes carbon and methane, which are classified as greenhouse gases. The technologies and facilities that are used to build renewable energy resources require fossil fuels, as do the transportation and distribution networks. In many instances, renewable energy relies on fossil fuels, whereas fossil fuels do not rely on renewables.
