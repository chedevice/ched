Meet the team

Stephen Ogunyiola
Co-Founder, CEO
Stephen serves as the CEO and the team Leader of Clean Hybrid Electric Device. As a Start-Up, We aim at solving the worlds energy and transportation problems through the provision of clean, affordable and reliable energy, generated by our revolutionary technology.

Our unique clean energy device will be part of the solution to global warming, weaning the energy industry off fossil fuel. For the consumer market, who need a clean, affordable and reliable energy source, our invention will provide a saving of 60% from their electricity bills. Other alternative energy solutions are not affordable by all, not scalable for everyday use, and not reliable. Therefore we have a big advantage.

As the CEO, Stephen leads the Start-up initiative through clean engineering, creative ideas, and user friendly design to develop revolutionary clean energy consumer products.

Stephen is also the creative and technical force behind the companys goal of ubiquitous, affordable, and clean global energy.
